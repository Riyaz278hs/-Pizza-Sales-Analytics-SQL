### Project Overview

This project delivers an end-to-end data analytics solution designed to evaluate a pizza store's operational efficiency and sales dynamics. By processing over 48,000 transaction records, the analysis extracts actionable business intelligence regarding consumer purchasing behavior, high-traffic bottlenecks.

pizza sales dashboard
           
        
### Data sources

 The core dataset is pizza_sales.csv, containing 48,620 detailed transactional records of individual pizza orders.

### Tech Stack & Tools
 
-  SQL Server (Transact-SQL): Advanced analytical querying, handling date/time extractions, and writing granular data aggregations.

-  MS Excel: Used for foundational structural profiling, rapid cross-verification, and data sanity checks.

### Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

   
-   Evaluated performance variation across hourly blocks and days of the week.
-  Segmented absolute sales density across distinct pizza sizes and categories.

### 📈Deep-Dive Insights

### Time & Traffic Hotspots

-  Hourly trends confirm massive transaction spikes between 5:00 PM and 8:00 PM.
-  Total order volume maximizes distinctly on Friday and Saturday evenings.

### Product Mix Dominance

-  The Classic category acts as the primary volume driver, leading with 26.91% of total sales.
-  Large (L) size pizzas dominate consumer preference, representing 45.89% of the entire store revenue matrix.
-  The Classic Deluxe Pizza achieved peak sales volume with 2,453 pizzas sold.
-  The Brie Carre Pizza recorded the lowest volume at 490 items sold.


### 💡Strategic Recommendations

- ⚡Staff for the Rush: Add extra staff on Fridays and Saturdays (5:00 PM – 8:00 PM) to fix dinner delays.  
- 🎯Clear Slow Inventory: Offer bundle deals on low-selling items like The Brie Carre Pizza to clear stock.  
- 📦Push Best Sellers: Feature Large Classic Pizzas heavily on menus since they drive the most revenue.

### 📊 Results

- 💰Total Revenue: $817,860.05  
- 🛒Total Orders: 21,350  
- 🍕Total Pizzas Sold: 49,574  
- 📈Average Order Value: $38.31  
- 🥇Top Category: Classic (Highest sales and revenue)  
- 📦Top Size: Large (L) (Accounts for 45.89% of all sales)  
- ⏰ Peak Traffic: Friday & Saturday evenings between 5:00 PM and 8:00 PM

### ⚠️Limitations

- 🧩No External Data: Lacks weather, holiday, or event data that naturally trigger sudden order spikes.  
- 🏷️No Discount History: Missing records of active coupons or promo deals, hiding price effects on sales.  
- 📍Single-Store Focus: Reflects performance for only one location, so trends cannot be generalized globally.
